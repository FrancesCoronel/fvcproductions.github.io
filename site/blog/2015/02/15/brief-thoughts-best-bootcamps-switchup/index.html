    Brief Thoughts on SwitchUp’s Best Bootcamps List

    Bootcamps are a risky business, make sure you make the right decision.

    6 minute read

    Created February 15, 2015

    0 views

    Full report can be found here.

    Tech Cocktail also went over it and ranked it by # of highest/most reviews. The Tech Cocktail list has 33 schools listed even though Switchup has 32 listed. I went with 32. Fullstack Academy got 3rd place! 😊

    A map of these bootcamps along with many others can be found here.

    Bootcamps are a risky business, make sure you make the right decision. I included the reviews that are up to date just for today.

    I think a website that showcases the reviews and star ratings from all the sites combined that automatically updates would be in place here…

    Also, I’d recommend checking out this Quora Has anyone had a bad experience at a programming bootcamp? and some Reddit threads for horror stories.
