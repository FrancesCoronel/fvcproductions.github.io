Frances Coronel is a software engineer specializing in UI development on the Customer Acquisition Team at Slack where her mission is to make your working life simpler, more pleasant and more productive.

She has been working professionally as a developer since 2015 and holds a Bachelors and Masters in Computer Science.

Outside of work, Frances is an Executive Director of Techqueria, a 501c3 nonprofit that serves the largest community of Latinx in Tech in the US.

In her free time, she enjoys contributing to open source, blogging and speaking at events.
